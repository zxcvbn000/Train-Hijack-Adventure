    A planned RPG 2D sidescrolling adventure game in early development.

    What is it about?

    You are a terrorist who has to make his way from Afghanistan to Europe, and hijack the Trans-European high speed rail at its peak hour. (not based on any real train lines). The train will make a wide and long turn around 70 miles in, which you will need to break into the cabin, and speed up the train in order to derail it and kill all passengers on board by rolling down the mountain.

    Interactive and adaptive AI

    Characters you meet in the game and your interaction with them will influence future outcomes in the game.

    Different characters and dynamic stats

    There will be different characters you can choose to play the game and your actions in the game will influence your character's stats, which will impact what he/she can do

    In-depth story and interactive plot

    The game will revolve around your choices.

    Failure and success.

    Based on your choices, you are able to loose the game in numerous ways, such as being caught by the police or running out time.
